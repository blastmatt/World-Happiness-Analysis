# World-Happiness-Analysis
Analysis of World Happiness Report Data provided by the Wellbeing Research Center at the University of Oxford.

In this project, I seek to explore a new approach to the WHR's concept of "dystopia," viewing it not only as a single unit to be used as a metric by which  are the citizens of countries are compared, but as a set of countries that contribute more data to their collective than just their lowest scores. This same approach allows the development of "Utopia," representing a collection of countries with traits that correlate to high "satisfaction" scores. Through these adjusted concepts, I provide in-depth analysis and visualization of trends that contribute to positive and negative living conditions in realistic quality of life extremes.
